☁️ Sync across devices
Uses a private GitHub Gist to sync your data between iPhone, iPad, and any other device. Your data stays private — only accessible with your personal token.
Get one free at github.com/settings/tokens — select gist scope only.
How it works: Push sends your data to GitHub. Pull fetches it on another device. Always Push from the device you just used, then Pull on the other device. Your token is stored locally on each device — never sent anywhere except GitHub.
